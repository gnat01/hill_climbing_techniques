# Codes in Python and R

## What Can Be Coded

### Python

- A generic GRASP engine with user-defined greedy construction and local search.
- Reactive GRASP variants that adapt $\alpha$ online.
- Benchmarks on set covering, TSP, and facility location.
- Per-iteration logs of construction quality versus local-search gain.

### R

- Compact pedagogical GRASP implementations with full experimental sweeps.
- Statistical summaries over many independent starts.
- Visualizations of solution-quality distributions under different RCL parameters.

## Extensions

- Reactive GRASP with adaptive $\alpha$.
- Path relinking from elite GRASP solutions.
- Hybrid GRASP plus tabu search or VNS in the local-search phase.
- Probabilistic RCL sampling weighted by greedy score.
- Parallel multi-start implementations with shared elite pools.
