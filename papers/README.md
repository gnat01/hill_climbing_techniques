# Papers Corpus

Initial seed corpus for this repo. The goal here is breadth across the most influential hill-climbing, local-search, and closely related metaheuristic methods. We can prune later.

## Simulated annealing and annealing foundations

1. Metropolis, N., Rosenbluth, A. W., Rosenbluth, M. N., Teller, A. H., Teller, E. (1953). *Equation of State Calculations by Fast Computing Machines*.
   Link: https://www.osti.gov/biblio/4390578
   DOI: https://doi.org/10.2172/4390578

2. Kirkpatrick, S., Gelatt, C. D., Vecchi, M. P. (1983). *Optimization by Simulated Annealing*.
   Link: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/17813860/
   DOI: https://doi.org/10.1126/science.220.4598.671

3. Geman, S., Geman, D. (1984). *Stochastic Relaxation, Gibbs Distributions, and the Bayesian Restoration of Images*.
   Link: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/22499653/
   DOI: https://doi.org/10.1109/TPAMI.1984.4767596

4. Cerny, V. (1985). *Thermodynamical Approach to the Traveling Salesman Problem: An Efficient Simulation Algorithm*.
   DOI: https://doi.org/10.1007/BF00940812
