# Codes in Python and R

## What Can Be Coded

### Python

- Simulated annealing for symmetric Euclidean TSP with swap, insert, and 2-opt-style moves.
- Efficient delta-evaluation routines for each neighborhood.
- Cooling schedule experiments on TSPLIB-style benchmark instances.
- Visual tour animations across the annealing run.

### R

- A teaching implementation for small and medium TSP instances.
- Comparative experiments across neighborhoods and schedule choices.
- Plotting of route length versus temperature and acceptance ratio.

## Extensions

- Hybrid annealing with deterministic 2-opt or Lin-Kernighan polishing.
- Adaptive move mixtures that change with temperature.
- Reheating when the chain stagnates.
- Extension to asymmetric TSP and time-window variants with penalty terms.
- Parallel multi-start annealing with cross-run statistics.
